Ganesh Chaturthi (September 7, Saturday): Ganesh Chaturthi will be celebrated in the Bhadrapada month on the Shukla Chaturthi Purnima. This is a propitious festival that commemorates the birthday of Lord Ganesha, known as the god of removal of obstacles.
